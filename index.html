 JBCN Cubing Association
A Rubik's Cube Intra-school championship

The JBCN Cubing Association was found by Jai Verma (Grade V) and Keisha George(Grade IV)in 2022 November. The debut competition was a huge success with 91 participants.

 JBCN Tune Wars
A 1V1 Band Music Tournament

JBCN Tune Wars was founded by Fiona Shah, Nishka Sah, Zoraan Rupani and Caleb Mehta in 2023. The debut competition for bands was between the Symphonic Troupe and the Adamcz Gig. The Teams were such:

  • Symphonic Troupe
  • Vocals-Samuel Dsouza,Nishka Sah,Nicole Ubabudike,Ruhhneet Dadiala
  • Guitar-Fiona Shah, Jai Verma
  • Keyboards-Adhrit Mishra,Swarit Shah
  • Adamcz Gig
  • Vocals-Caleb Mehta, Dhruv Suryawanshi,Asmi Haldankar
  • Guitar-Gabriel Rodrigues, Gautami Jhawar
  • Keyboard- Zoraan Rupani, Avneesh Phusalkar

  • Symphonic Troupe won by 2.5 points (13.5/11)
